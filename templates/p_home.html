Healthcare Services

Hospitals are institutions designed to provide medical, surgical, and nursing care for the sick or injured. They offer a wide range of healthcare services, including emergency care, diagnostics, treatment, surgery, and rehabilitation.

Staffing

Hospitals employ various healthcare professionals, including doctors, nurses, technicians, therapists, pharmacists, and administrative staff. Each plays a crucial role in ensuring patients receive comprehensive care.

Emergency Services

Hospitals have emergency departments (EDs) to handle urgent medical situations, such as trauma, accidents, heart attacks, and strokes. These departments operate 24/7 and are staffed to respond to medical emergencies promptly.

Patient Care

Hospitals prioritize patient care and safety. They adhere to strict protocols and standards to provide quality care and minimize the risk of medical errors or infections.
